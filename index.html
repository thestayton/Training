A/B rotation logic
A
Week 1: A · B · A — two strength sessions, one volume
B
Week 2: B · A · B — two volume sessions, one strength
↻
Repeats every 2 weeks. Over a 16-week block: 24 sessions total, 12 of each type.
!
Each main lift gets 2× weekly stimulus — front squat, deadlift pattern, pull-up, press all appear in both sessions.
Decision rules
▲
Busy week — drop 1 set per lift. Keep all movements. Don't skip the session.
→
Calm week — push reps and load. This is your window.
!
Joint irritation — swap variation immediately. Keep progressing the substitute.
~
Eccentric focus — 2–3 sec down on 1–2 lifts per session for tendon health (Baar).
↓
Session RPE 8–9 two sessions in a row — deload incoming. Cut sets, hold load.
